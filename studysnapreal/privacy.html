Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Snapwize ("we," "us," "our") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Information you provide

When you use Snapwize, you may submit topics you type or photos of notes you upload. These are used only to generate your study materials.

2. Information stored on your device

Snapwize saves your study sets, settings, and daily usage count in your browser's local storage. This information stays on your device — it is not sent to or stored by us. Clearing your browser data will remove it.

3. How your study content is processed

To generate study materials, the topics and images you submit are sent to our AI provider (Groq) for processing. They are used only to create your study materials. We do not sell your content, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. Accounts

Snapwize does not currently require an account to use its core features, and we do not collect names or passwords for everyday use.

5. Payments

If you purchase a paid plan, your payment is handled by our payment processor, Stripe. Snapwize does not see or store your full card details. Stripe processes your payment information under its own privacy policy.

6. Advertising

The Free plan displays advertising through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can review and control ad personalisation through your Google account settings. Paid plans (Pro and Premium) display no ads.

7. Hosting

Snapwize is hosted on Vercel, which may process basic technical information (such as IP addresses) as part of normal website operation.

8. Children's privacy

Snapwize is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your choices

You can clear all of your study data at any time from the Settings page in the app, or by clearing your browser's storage.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.

11. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Contact us at 1maddoxcole.1@gmail.com.
